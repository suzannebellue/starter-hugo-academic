I am an Assistant Professor of Economics at [CREST - ENSAE - Institut Polytechnique de Paris](https://www.ensae.fr/en/research/center-research-economics-and-statistics-crest) and I hold a Ph.D. from [Mannheim University](https://www.vwl.uni-mannheim.de/en/) (2023). I am also a Research Affiliate at [RFBerlin](https://www.rfberlin.com) and a Research Affiliate in Macroeconomics and Growth at the [Center for Economic Policy Research (CEPR)](https://cepr.org/research/programme-areas/macroeconomics-and-growth).

My research spans family macroeconomics and development economics, with a focus on the causes and consequences of inequality and social mobility.
